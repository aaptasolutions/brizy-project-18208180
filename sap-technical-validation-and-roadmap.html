SAP technical validation and roadmap

Removal of redundant technical coding

Our easy to install program is designed to seamlessly identify and integrate custom fields, validations, and other specific development rules in the background. This adaptive feature removes the need to create a minimum of 5 to 7 separate programs on an average. As a result, consultants are relieved from the time-consuming tasks of drafting functional specifications, technical specifications, mapping data, coding, conducting extensive testing, and organizing transportation processes.

 

The significant advantage of our product is that it expedites the development process. By eliminating the lengthy process of creating multiple programs, our solution ensures that these programs are operational right from the beginning of the project, on day 1. This immediate availability is crucial because it means there's no need to wait until day end of the project to observe how data will be displayed and transformed within the new system. This not only saves valuable time but also enhances efficiency and accelerates the overall progress of the project.

Holistic Project Assessments:

In today's dynamic business landscape, projects often span multiple years and involve extensive resources. The ever-shifting nature of the business world necessitates constant adaptation for IT projects. The risk of scope creep and obsolescence looms large, making it crucial to evaluate projects functionally, technically, and managerially. This evaluation ensures that projects remain aligned with their original objectives and continue delivering the intended value envisioned during their conception.

We assist our clients by conducting thorough evaluations, approaching the assessment from multiple angles:

Functional Perspective

We scrutinize the functional aspects to guarantee alignment with project goals and objectives.

Technical Analysis

Our experts assess the technical aspects, ensuring the project remains technologically relevant and efficient.

Managerial Assessment

We evaluate the managerial aspects, including project management strategies and resource utilization, to maintain effective leadership and coordination.

 

Our seasoned professionals engage in onsite workshops with the project leadership team, key process owners, and core resources.
